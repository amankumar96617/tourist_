About Destination

Jankinagar is a town located in the Madhubani district of Bihar, India. It is part of the Mithila region, which is known for its rich cultural heritage, traditional art, and vibrant festivals. Jankinagar is closely connected to the Maithili culture and language, reflecting the customs and traditions of this historic area.
