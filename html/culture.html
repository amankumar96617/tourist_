Famous Bihar Cultural Festivals

Chhath Puja

Sun worship festival celebrated with devotion across Bihar.

Buddha Jayanti

Celebration of Buddha's birth and teachings, majorly in Gaya.

Madhubani Art

Famous painting style from Mithila region, colorful and traditional.

Guru Govind Singh Jayanti

Guru Gobind Singh Jayanti is one of the most sacred festivals of the Sikh community.

Diwali

Diwali is a major Indian festival that symbolizes the victory of light over darkness and good over evil.

Folk Dance

Traditional dances like Jat-Jatin, Bidesia, and Chhau performed across Bihar.

Muharram

Observed widely in Bihar with processions and cultural rituals by the Muslim community.

Eid

Bihar’s Muslim community celebrates Eid with grand feasts and communal prayers.
